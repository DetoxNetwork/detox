We are Detox, an assortment of useful websites to help you in school or your day-to-day life. Our collection is constantly growing and improving, aiming to provide you the best experience possible. Here at the homepage, we feature our best sites and projects, so far being Evade, subLink, and Phantom.

Evade

Evade is a collection of bookmarklets that allows you to bypass any school or work web filter. Simply drag the code into your bookmarks bar, then click on it while on a webpage. It will activate the code and you will never be bored in school again!


subLink

subLink is an intuitive web proxy that has multiple options as which way you will bypass web filters. To use subLink, enter a link, select the proxy you want to use, and hit enter. subLink has two modes: normal and incognito.


Phantom

Phantom is a way to disable any extension installed by your administrator. Simply click on the bookmark, and you will be brought to a page to enable or disable anything. This does not work for installing new extensions.
